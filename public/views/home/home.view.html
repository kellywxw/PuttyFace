  • Before PuttyFace

    We all know that making things is hard because this is what we do all day, and we understand the dedication required to make something great.

    But what if it was wasn’t?

  • PuttyFace is Born!

    We call it PuttyFace and we hope you enjoy it.

  • What is PuttyFace?

    PuttyFace imports any 3D object and allows you to sculpt it with your hands or controller in VR. You can play, share, add emoji stickers, or print your object with a simple and intuitive UI just as your would play-doh. The results are unique and require no technical background to get started. Its perfect for kids and adults alike and is highly addictive.
